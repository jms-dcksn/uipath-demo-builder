# Research And Citation Rules

## 1) Source Quality

- Prefer primary sources: official docs, regulators, standards bodies, platform docs.
- Use secondary sources only when primary coverage is limited.
- Avoid anonymous or low-credibility content for core process assumptions.

## 2) Minimum Research Depth

- At least 5 relevant sources per use case.
- At least 2 sources that describe real operational workflows in the target domain.
- At least 1 source on compliance/risk constraints when applicable.

## 3) Citation Format

Use this compact format:

- `[SRC-001] Title, Publisher, URL, Accessed YYYY-MM-DD`

## 4) Claim Tagging

- Tag critical claims with one or more source IDs.
- Mark inferred statements as `Inference` when not directly stated in sources.

## 5) Freshness

- For changing topics (regulations, product features, pricing), verify recency before finalizing recommendations.
- Record access date for each source.
